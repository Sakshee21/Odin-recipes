Ingredients

  1. 2 cups cooked rice (preferably leftover or cooled rice)
  2. 2 tablespoons oil (vegetable or sesame oil)
  3. 1/2 cup carrots (finely chopped or julienned)
  4. 1/2 cup green peas (fresh or frozen)
  5. 1/2 cup capsicum (bell pepper) (finely chopped)
  6. 1/4 cup spring onions (finely chopped, white and green parts separated)
  7. 1/4 cup cabbage (shredded, optional)
  8. 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  9. 1 teaspoon vinegar (optional, for tanginess)
  10. 1/2 teaspoon black pepper powder
  11. 1 teaspoon salt (adjust to taste)
  12. 1 teaspoon chili sauce (optional, for spiciness)
  13. 2 tablespoons spring onion greens (chopped)
